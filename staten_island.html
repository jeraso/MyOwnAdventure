STATEN ISLAND

Staten Island has many historical sites under the Staten Island Historical Society. Fort Wadsworth is located by The Narrows and the Staten Island Ferry brings you one of the best photo ops of the Statue of Liberty. The Verrazano Bridge is the only road connection to the rest of New York City.
